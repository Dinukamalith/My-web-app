Manage Your Time Effectively

Set Clear Goals

Define what you want to achieve during your study sessions. Break larger tasks into smaller, manageable goals.

Create a Study Schedule

Plan your study time in advance. Allocate specific time slots for different subjects and stick to your schedule.

Prioritize Tasks

Identify the most important tasks and tackle them first. Use methods like the Eisenhower Matrix to prioritize effectively.

Take Regular Breaks

Incorporate short breaks into your study routine. This helps refresh your mind and maintain productivity.

Limit Distractions

Create a conducive study environment by minimizing distractions. Put your phone away and focus on your studies.
